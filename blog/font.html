I changed my fonts...

If you're thinking, what the hell is this guy talking about? He changed his fonts, WHO THE HELL CARES??!?!? Uh, good point.

But, I think this deserves a blog post.

For the longest time, I've had the same font for headings and body text, and that didn't bother me. The font was originally Basiic, but that changed to Ticketing (but if you look at the src of this website it still says Basiic :P).

But I've been thinking around changing the system for a while now. And, I did it!

The new fonts look ... modern - new visual style?

Nope! Hell no way I'm ever changing the pixel-y retro style of my website. It suits me perfectly and I'm never changing the distinct style of my website. While I am planning to add some silly new features, the underlying visual style will stay the same.

What are the fonts?

Heading: Ticketing (my ol' favourite)
Body: Rubik (my favourite sans-serif font)
Blog: Source Serif 4 (it's a good font)
